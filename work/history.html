    • Croatias History

    Croatia have a difficult history when it comes to the euros, This is because they half the time struggle to qualify or make it past the knockout stages, This can be proven by the previous results being:

      EURO-by-EURO record
    • 1996: Quarter-finals
    • 2000: Did not qualify
    • 2004: Group stage
    • 2008: Quarter-finals
    • 2012: Group stage
    • 2016: Round of 16
    • 2020: Round of 16

    However there are positives to croatia taking parts in these euro games, This is because they have got a good goal ratio and previous results against difficult sides some of these include:
